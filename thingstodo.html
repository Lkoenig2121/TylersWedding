Things To Do!

In the time between the ceremony and cocktail hour while the bridal party are taking pictures, we invite you to explore Red Bank. Just make sure to make it back to Molly Pitcher Inn by 5:30pm for cocktail hour!

Some activities we recommend:

  • Talk a walk by the water and enjoy the Navesink River at Riverside Gardens Park on West Front Street or Marine Park located on Wharf Avenue
  • Enjoy some shopping at one of the stores in Red Bank
  • Shop at one of the many boutiques or stores in town like Urban Outfitters, Jack's Record Shoppe, Greene Street consignment shop, Dor L'Dor clothing shop and the Monmouth Street Emporium antique store
  • Have some fun playing a few games of pinball at Yestercades or looking at some comics at Jay & Silent Bob's Secret Stash
  • Check out some art at the Beacon Fine Arts Gallery or the Chetkin Gallery
  • Or head over early and enjoy the ambiance of the Molly Pitcher
